Healthcare Guardian Onboarding

Welcome to Healthcare Guardian!
Follow the steps below to start tracking your health data:
Step 1: Download Google Fit
Download and install the Google Fit app from the App Store or Google Play Store.
Step 2: Sign Up with Google Fit
Open the Google Fit app and sign up or log in with your Google account.
Step 3: Allow Permissions for Google Fit
Grant necessary permissions within the Google Fit app to sync your health data.
Step 4: Connect Apple Health Data
Open the Apple Health app on your device and navigate to the data sharing settings.
Step 5: Login to Healthcare Guardian
Log in to Healthcare Guardian using your Google account and grant permissions to access your health data.
Congratulations!
You have successfully completed the onboarding process.
