Healthcare Guardian Onboarding

Welcome to Healthcare Guardian!
Follow the steps below to start tracking your health data:
Step 1: Download Google Fit
Download and install the Google Fit app from the App Store or Google Play Store.

iOS

Android

Step 2: Sign Up\login to Google Fit
Open the Google Fit app and sign up or log in with your Google account.

Google Fit Help

Step 3: Allow Permissions for Google Fit
Grant necessary permissions within the Google Fit app to sync your health data.
Step 4: Connect Fit App with Existing Smart watch App to sync your watch data
Follo below steps if you are using ios and your watch connected to Apple Health.
  1. On your iPhone, open the Google Fit app.
  2. At the bottom, tap Profile.
  3. At the top, tap Settings.
  4. Under "Activity Tracking," tap Track your activities with Health.
  5. Turn on Let Google Fit access Health.
  6. If necessary, follow the instructions to sync Fit with Health.
Follow the below steps if using Non iOS or Android.
  1. On your phone, open the Google Fit app.
  2. At the bottom, tap Profile.
  3. At the top, tap Settings.
  4. Under "Activity Tracking," tap Track your activities with Your existing Health App.
  5. Turn on Let Google Fit access Health.
  6. If necessary, follow the instructions to sync Fit with existing App.
Step 5: Login to Healthcare Guardian
Log in to Healthcare Guardian using your Google account and grant permissions to access your health data.
Congratulations!
You have successfully completed the onboarding process.
